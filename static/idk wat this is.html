Free Screening of Hidden Figures

May 5th - 6pm

Ohlone College's ./ComputerScience, SWE, and A^2 Pi are colaborating to bring you a FREE screening of the award wining film Hidden Figures. Make sure to come watch this inspiring film. Hidden Figures depicts the obstacles that many women and minorities have, and still are overcoming, within the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) field. Today's push for equality is of growing importance!

Yes, this event will have food! Come hungry, discounts will be granted to those bearing Ohlone ID's. Pizza, popcorn, candy, soda, etc. will be availible for purchase.

There will also be a raffle! Must attend to win, contact kaishin@dot-slash-cs.club or contact us on facebook for tickets. Tickets also availiable during the event.

The exact location is still TBD, but it will be on Ohlone Fremont campus. So be sure to bring $4 for parking, or be prepared to go on a short walk from a public parking location.

Please come out and join the Ohlone Community in pushing for equality in STEM. For any question feel free or wish to provide any input, such can be done at cin@dot-slash-cs.club.
