About

At TreeIO, our mission is to create a robust, yet elegant interface for organizations to unify sub communities under one domain while providing the means to emphasize their individuality. Group specific communication should not come at the expense of oranizational unity and vice versa. We strive to make sub-communities more accesible through a large organization. This platform is idealized for schools with clubs, organizations with chapters, etc.
